Kulfie

Kulfi Bar India

The difference between kulfi and ice cream is what they’re made of: A standard ice cream recipe typically uses eggs, whereas kulfi is an eggless ice cream. The lack of eggs in kulfi creates a creamy frozen dessert that isn’t as airy or fluffy as ice cream.An absolutely wonderful Kulfi. One of my favorite desert now found. I like to twist it up a bit sometimes and add chocolate and cocoa to the mix.
