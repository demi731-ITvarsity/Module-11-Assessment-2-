Tuscan Chicken Casserole

La Kouzina Greece

Baked Tuscan Chicken Casserole – So quick and flavorful. Everyone will love this delicious chicken casserole. This easy baked Tuscan chicken casserole meal combines spinach, garlic, sun-dried tomatoes, cream cheese, and parmesan and comes together quite quickly for a busy day’s meal. Just like our popular spinach chicken casserole, this Tuscan chicken casserole recipe is also keto-friendly, gluten-free, and low carb.
